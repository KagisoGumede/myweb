My Story

Where I Come From

I was born at Manguzi Hospital, KwaZulu-Natal — deep in a rural area where electricity was a luxury we simply did not have. My childhood was not easy, but it was rich in lessons that no classroom could ever teach.

Every morning I would wake up before sunrise and walk more than 5 kilometres to school and back in the afternoon — rain or shine. After school, life did not stop. There were cows to care for, crops to grow, and a community to feed. We grew our own food and sold produce locally — that was my first lesson in supply, demand, and understanding people's real needs.

I grew up surrounded by love — my grandmother, grandfather, father, and extended family who poured wisdom into me every single day. They taught me how to overcome challenges, how to be resilient, and that your circumstances do not define your ceiling.

It was in those years that I discovered a hidden gift — I could play the drums. Church became the stage where that talent grew. Every Sunday, the rhythm section was mine. Music taught me discipline, timing, and how to be part of something bigger than yourself — lessons I carry into every project I work on today.

The Journey

My Life Timeline

Early Years

🌿 Born in Manguzi, KwaZulu-Natal

Raised in a rural community with no electricity. Life revolved around family, farming, livestock, and the church. These humble beginnings built the foundation of who I am — adaptable, hardworking, and deeply grateful.

Primary School

📖 Mengu Primary School

Started my formal education at Mengu Primary School — walking 5km each way through rural roads every single day. Curiosity was always my strongest subject. I wanted to understand how and why everything worked.

High School

🎓 Nodineka High School — Matric 2021

Completed my National Senior Certificate in Science and Mathematics. This was a major turning point — I knew technology and problem-solving were my future. I left with a certificate and a hunger to see the world beyond the rural hills.

University

💻 Diploma in Informatics — TUT 2022–2024

Moved to Johannesburg and enrolled at Tshwane University of Technology. Studied Systems Analysis, Database Management, Business Analysis, and Software Engineering. The rural kid with no electricity discovered he could build digital systems for thousands of people.

First Role

☁️ Business/Systems Analyst Intern — Rain Cloud 2024

Landed my first professional role — an 8-month internship where I documented 150+ requirements, designed 40+ UML diagrams, and worked across 3 enterprise systems. From growing crops to architecting data. The journey was becoming real.

Present Day

🏦 Software Developer Learnership — FNB 2024–Now

Today I build banking systems at one of South Africa's biggest banks — serving 500+ daily users, writing Spring Boot APIs, and leading BA/SA functions. From no electricity in KZN to powering digital finance in Johannesburg. The boy from Manguzi made it.
